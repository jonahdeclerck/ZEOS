Introduction

Operational manual for the ZEOS Combat Simulator.

This documentation provides technical specifications and setup procedures for ZEOS FPV systems. Use the navigation on the left to configure your hardware or verify your flight kit for field deployment.

Setup Guide

Hardware configuration and simulator mission launch.

1. Controller Setup

1.1 Physical Connection

Connect your RC controller to your laptop using the provided usb-c to usb-c cable, and power up the controller by sliding the power switch, on the bottom of the controller, to the right. Most modern controllers are recognized automatically as joystick input—check “Game Controllers” to confirm before using simulators.

1.2 Access Controls Menu

In the startup menu, press the CONTROLS button to enter control settings to set up your controller.

1.3 Calibration Method

Select the correct input device (look for terms like "USB Gamepad","Joystick", "HID Device" or the brand of your controller) and press ASSISTED CALIBRATION to enter the guided calibration. Later, if that doesn't suffice, use MANUAL CALIBRATION to tailor settings to your specific controller.

1.4 Finalize Calibration

Press CALIBRATE to start the guided calibration process and follow on-screen instructions to complete it. after this, you will be presented with a visualization of your controller inputs, as well as a drone model, reacting to them, allowing you to verify that all channels are responding correctly.

2. Goggles Integration

2.1 Laptop Port Preparation

Make sure the HDMI adapter on the Framework Laptop is correctly installed, we will be plugging the FPV Goggles into this.

2.2 Wiring and Power

Use the Micro HDMI to HDMI Cable to connect the ORQA FPV Goggles' Micro HDMI port to the Framework laptop's HDMI port. For power, connect either the provided 9V Barrel Jack to USB-A cable or the standalone battery.

3. Training Launch

3.1 Access Training Menu

From the main menu, click the TRAINING button to view available simulation scenarios.

3.2 Select Scenario

Choose your desired training type from the list. For this example, select One Way Attack Demo 1.

3.3 Environment & Launch

On the right side of the screen, you can adjust the Weather and Time settings. Once configured, click the START button at the bottom right to launch the simulator.

4. Shutdown

Laptop Shutdown

The system is configured to respond to Ctrl + Alt + Delete to trigger an easy instant shutdown.
